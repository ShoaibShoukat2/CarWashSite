Savings

Customers can save between 65% and 70% compared to individual washes. The time saved by not having to travel to a car wash or wait in lines is invaluable.

Purchase Process

Customers can register on the web or the AWA app by selecting the type of vehicle (compact, sedan, SUV, or truck) and then the desired subscription plan (annual, semi-annual, monthly, or single wash). Monthly and semi-annual subscription plans allow adding categories, while the annual subscription includes premium access.

Payment Methods

Customers can pay using QR, credit, or debit cards.
